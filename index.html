How To Play


The objective of this game is to get from your current page to the other page specified using only in-site links on Wikipedia. You may not search.


About Us


Our names are Marko and Brady. We enjoy coding and making websites and apps that offer people entertainment and knowledge. This made WikiFind a no-brainer. We had played WikiFind on Wikipedia's actual site before by coming up with our own starting and ending pages. However, we wanted a site that would auto-generate these pages for us. Thus, WikiFind was born.
